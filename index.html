Riemann Zeta Function Connection

The probability $P$ that two random integers $a, b$ are coprime is governed by the value of the **Riemann Zeta function** at $s=2$.

The density of coprime pairs is $P = \frac{1}{\zeta(2)} = \frac{6}{\pi^2} \approx 0.6079 $.
